About Us

At 'Stray Dog Welfare NGO', we are dedicated to improving the lives of stray dogs. Our mission is to provide love, care, and a safe haven for these homeless and helpless creatures.

Our vision is to increase the adoption rate of stray dogs in India by 30% by 2040. We believe every dog deserves a loving home, and we are working tirelessly to achieve this goal.

Our Services & Programs

Food & Shelter

We provide regular food and safe living spaces for stray dogs.

Medical Care & Vaccination

We treat sick and injured dogs and ensure regular vaccinations.

Adoption & Awareness

We run adoption campaigns and raise awareness among people.
